3. DMCA Takedown Request

If you believe that content accessible through our website infringes your copyright, you may submit a DMCA takedown notice. To be effective, the notification must include:

  • A physical or electronic signature of the copyright owner or authorized agent

  • Your contact information (address, telephone number, and email)
  • A statement that you have a good faith belief that use of the material is not authorized
  • A statement, under penalty of perjury, that the information is accurate and that you are authorized to act on behalf of the copyright owner

5. Counter-Notification
